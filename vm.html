Visual Media

Overview

The Visual Media Committee will guide you through the creative side of business and enhance your technical skills primarily in cinematography and photography utilizing Adobe Creative Suite (PremierePro and Lightroom). You will capture priceless memories during PBL’s professional and social events, and express them in the best light with your photos and videos. You will also pick up people management skills along the way by working closely with members across PBL.

Each of you will get the chance to explore the integration of art into business by creating your own portfolio, and to build transferable leadership skills by managing multiple projects throughout the semester! You don’t need any prior photography or videography experience to apply, because we will teach you everything you need to know! Join us if you are passionate, creative, and willing to learn!.
